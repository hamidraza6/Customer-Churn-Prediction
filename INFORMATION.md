### ⚠️ Common GitHub Errors & Fixes

| Error | Fix |
|---|---|
| `git is not recognized` | Install Git from https://git-scm.com |
| `Authentication failed` | Use a Personal Access Token instead of password |
| `File too large (100MB)` | Add `model.pkl` and `scaler.pkl` to `.gitignore` |
| `remote origin already exists` | Run `git remote set-url origin YOUR_URL` |
| `rejected - non-fast-forward` | Run `git pull origin main --allow-unrelated-histories` then push again |

## 📝 Prediction Options Explained

Here is a breakdown of all the fields you need to fill out in the **Predict** section:

### 👤 Account Information
- **Tenure (Months):** The total number of months the customer has stayed with the company.
- **Monthly Charges ($):** The amount charged to the customer on a monthly basis.
- **Total Charges ($):** The total accumulated amount charged to the customer so far.

### 👥 Demographics
- **Gender:** The customer's gender (Male or Female).
- **Senior Citizen:** Whether the customer is 65 years or older (Yes or No).
- **Partner:** Whether the customer is married or lives with a partner (Yes or No).
- **Dependents:** Whether the customer has children or other dependents living with them (Yes or No).

### 📡 Services Subscribed
- **Phone Service:** Whether the customer has a primary landline/phone service (Yes or No).
- **Multiple Lines:** Whether the customer has multiple telephone lines (Yes, No, or No Phone Service).
- **Internet Service:** The type of internet connection the customer uses (DSL, Fiber Optic, or No Internet).
- **Online Security:** Premium security add-on to protect against viruses/malware (Yes, No, or No Internet).
- **Online Backup:** Cloud storage add-on for backing up data (Yes, No, or No Internet).
- **Device Protection:** Insurance/protection plan for their equipment (Yes, No, or No Internet).
- **Tech Support:** Priority technical support subscription (Yes, No, or No Internet).
- **Streaming TV:** Whether the customer uses the network for streaming television (Yes, No, or No Internet).
- **Streaming Movies:** Whether the customer uses the network for streaming movies (Yes, No, or No Internet).

### 📄 Contract & Billing
- **Contract Type:** The billing agreement term (Month-to-Month, One Year, or Two Year).
- **Paperless Billing:** Whether the customer receives bills via email instead of physical mail (Yes or No).
- **Payment Method:** How the customer pays their bill (Bank Transfer, Credit Card, Electronic Check, or Mailed Check).
