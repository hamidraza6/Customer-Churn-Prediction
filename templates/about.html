About the Project

Behind ChurnAI

Understanding the technology and the mission.

Mission Statement

Our goal is to help businesses retain their most valuable asset: their customers. By leveraging predictive analytics, we empower organizations to identify at-risk customers early and take proactive steps to improve satisfaction and loyalty.

Customer churn can severely impact revenue. However, identifying the signals of churn manually is almost impossible given the large volume of data. ChurnAI solves this by analyzing usage patterns, billing information, and demographic data in real-time.

  • Proactive Action: Intervene before the customer leaves.
  • Data-Driven: Make decisions based on robust machine learning models.
  • Scalable: Designed to process large datasets quickly.

The Team

This application was developed as a robust End-to-End Machine Learning project, demonstrating the integration of a trained Random Forest classifier within a modern, responsive Flask web application.
